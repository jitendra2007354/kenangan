Manuscript Guidelines and Inquiries

Manuscript Submissions

The Kenangan Journal accepts unsolicited policy briefs, data-driven structural analyses, and systematic reviews. Manuscripts must be formatted according to the APA 7th Edition guidelines and submitted in PDF format.

To submit a manuscript for peer review, please email the Editorial Office with your abstract and CV attached.
