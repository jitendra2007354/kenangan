About the Journal

Aims, Scope, and Editorial Board

Mission Statement

The Kenangan Journal of Structural Policy & Infrastructure is an independent, peer-reviewed digital publication dedicated to rigorous, data-driven analysis of national policy frameworks. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic research and actionable public administration, providing stakeholders with authoritative insights into technological frontiers, cultural economics, and sovereign infrastructure.

Editorial Policy & Peer Review

Kenangan adheres to strict academic standards for publication. All submitted policy briefs and structural analyses undergo a rigorous double-blind peer-review process. Submissions are evaluated based on:

  • Empirical rigor and data provenance
  • Structural methodology and systemic reasoning
  • Originality of strategic resolution vectors

We maintain absolute editorial independence and are committed to publishing objective, non-partisan research.
