IPL 2026: The Rise of the 'New Six Machine' in Rajasthan

Written by Aria Kenangan • May 30, 2026

The 2026 Indian Premier League season has delivered an abundance of electrifying cricket, but no story has captured the nation's imagination quite like the meteoric rise of Vaibhav Sooryavanshi. Playing for the Rajasthan Royals, his astonishing knock of 97 runs recently sent Sunrisers Hyderabad home and pushed Rajasthan one step closer to the final.

Eclipsing the Legends

Though he narrowly missed a century, Sooryavanshi's innings was nothing short of historic. He officially broke Chris Gayle's iconic IPL record for the most sixes hit in a single season. The raw power and fearless stroke play demonstrated by the young batter represent a massive generational shift in Indian T20 cricket.

"We are witnessing the birth of a new six machine. The sheer bat speed and confidence are unprecedented."
— Chris Gayle on the 2026 Season

Looking Towards the Finale

With Rajasthan Royals advancing to Qualifier 2, the energy across the state is palpable. From heated rivalries to lighthearted banter—like Jofra Archer's recent digs at PBKS—the tournament continues to be the ultimate summer carnival. As the nation gears up for the finals, all eyes remain firmly on Sooryavanshi.
